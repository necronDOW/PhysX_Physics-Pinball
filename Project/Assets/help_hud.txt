 Simulation
    F9 - select next actor
    F10 - pause
    F12 - reset

 Display
    F5 - help on/off
    F6 - shadows on/off
    F7 - render mode

 Camera
    W,S,A,D,Q,Z - forward,backward,left,right,up,down
    mouse + click - change orientation
    F8 - reset view

 Force (applied to selected actor)
    I,K,J,L,U,M - forward,backward,left,right,up,down
